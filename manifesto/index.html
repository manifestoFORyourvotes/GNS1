Manifesto for Financial Secretary

Hey there, fellow students of Delsu,

It is with both humility and enthusiasm that I stand before you today as a candidate for the esteemed position of Financial Secretary. I am not here to offer you empty promises, but rather a vision—a vision where the management of our school’s finances is characterized by transparency, equity, and a little bit of flair. Yes, I believe that even the art of budgeting can be exciting and engaging if done with the right spirit. Transparency and equity are the pillars upon which we will build a future that is both efficient and collaborative. Financial management isn’t just about numbers, it’s about community, responsibility, and a shared vision of what’s possible. I believe that by making transparent decisions, we can ensure everyone has a clear understanding of how funds are allocated, and that no student feels left out or unaware of the decisions being made. This is the kind of open leadership I will bring to this position. Read more

What I Offer You:

  • Transparency: You shall never be left in the dark about the allocation of funds. Every decision, every expenditure will be laid bare, ensuring that no penny is spent without your knowledge. After all, openness is the cornerstone of trust.
  • Fairness: Whether you’re the captain of a team, the president of a club, or a student with a great idea, I promise you this: all voices shall be heard, and no one shall be overlooked. Your needs will be considered with the utmost seriousness, and funds will be distributed accordingly. Equity will guide my decisions. This means that no student or group should feel like their voice is ignored. Every opportunity will be given a fair chance to thrive. I believe we can create a system where every idea, no matter how big or small, is given the attention it deserves. I am committed to making sure that all students have an equal say in how our resources are distributed and used. Read more
  • Accountability: Just as the weight of responsibility rests upon the shoulders of a student body, so too will I bear the weight of the financial integrity entrusted to me. Every expense will be meticulously tracked, ensuring that our collective resources are handled with the care they deserve. I will ensure that every dollar spent is justified, tracked, and used to its full potential for the benefit of all students. It’s not just about managing funds, it’s about being accountable to you—the students—who entrust me with this role. My approach will be one of vigilance and transparency, so you can always trust that our finances are being handled responsibly. Read more
  • Fundraising with Finesse: Gone will be the days of tedious bake sales and predictable raffles. Instead, we shall elevate our fundraising efforts with creative ideas—perhaps a talent show to remember, a thrilling dodgeball tournament, or even an auction that unites us in both purpose and fun. Funds will be raised with passion, and not without a sense of adventure! I will ensure that every fundraising event is something memorable—something that brings the whole school together in a spirit of fun and community. Fundraising can and should be exciting! No more cookie-cutter events—let's get creative and raise funds in ways that make everyone excited to participate! Read more
  • Financial Literacy: I firmly believe that understanding the flow of money—both within our school and in the world beyond—is a vital skill for us all. As such, I will work to bring financial literacy workshops to our community, equipping us all with the knowledge to manage resources wisely, both as a school and as individuals.
  • Humility: While I stand here seeking your support, I do so not as a self-proclaimed leader but as a fellow student who is deeply invested in making our school a better place. I approach this position not with pride, but with the understanding that it is a privilege to serve you. Together, we shall shape a future that is inclusive, thoughtful, and efficient. Leadership is not about commanding from the front, but walking alongside you—listening to you, collaborating with you, and making sure that all of us have a stake in our collective success. I am here to serve, not to lead from above. I will always remain humble and open to your feedback, because your voice matters in every decision I make. Read more

Why I Seek Your Vote:

  • Because I believe in fairness, and will ensure that every student, regardless of background or position, is given an equal opportunity to benefit from our shared resources.
  • Because I am committed to listening—to your concerns, to your ideas, and to your hopes for our school’s future. Your voice will not only be heard, it will be heeded.
  • Because I believe that when we work together, when we act in concert, we can achieve great things. I am here not to dictate, but to collaborate, to serve, and to ensure that our collective goals are realized.

Let us, together, make every penny count—and in doing so, create a legacy of transparency, fairness, and excitement for the future!

Awala Blessing. A
Candidate for Financial Secretary
